Chapter 1: "Silver in the Hallway"

[BG: Seiran Academy - Classroom 2-A, Morning] [BGM: "School Chimes" - calm, routine]

NARRATOR (KAITO): Two weeks since I found the Door. My life has split into two distinct shapes. In one, I am hunting dire wolves in a forest older than history. In the other, I am trying to explain to Elia Von Astraea why she cannot bring a sword to Home Economics.

NARRATOR (KAITO): Kage is growing. He is now the size of a wolfhound. I tell neighbors he is a rare foreign breed. This is technically true. The breed is "Fenrir."

NARRATOR (KAITO): Elia has enrolled at Seiran under Airi's arrangement. Her assimilation has been... enthusiastic. She has broken three smartphones in four days. She accidentally challenged the history teacher to a formal debate regarding the geopolitical structure of feudal Japan, which she won. Yesterday she organized a "diplomatic breakfast" in the school garden that somehow involved forty students and a tablecloth she brought from Astraea.

[SFX: Classroom door sliding open]

The teacher enters. Behind him, a girl. Silver hair, cut sharp. Eyes that are grey and do not blink often enough. She stands with a stillness that makes the rest of the class look frantic by comparison.

TEACHER: "Class, we have a transfer student. This is Shirogane Seira. Please welcome her."

She bows. It is precise. 15 degrees. Efficiency over etiquette.

SHIRA: "Shirogane Seira. Please take care of me."

NARRATOR (KAITO): She scanned the room once. Left to right. She checked the windows. She checked the rear exit. Then her eyes stopped on me. For half a second. Then she moved on.

YUI: (whispering to Ryuusei) "New girl. Silver hair. Either a spy or an assassin or both."

RYUUSEI: "Why?"

YUI: "Amane-kun clocked her in thirty seconds and hasn't looked back since. Classic evasion. He knows. She knows he knows. This is cinema."

[SCENE BREAK: Lunchtime, Rooftop]

Kaito sits on the bench. He opens his bento. He is alone. Then he isn't. Shirogane Seira is standing by the fence, looking at the courtyard below.

KAITO: "You're watching the exits."

She doesn't turn around immediately. She finishes her scan.

SHIRA: "You noticed."

KAITO: "You're also watching me."

SHIRA: "Yes."

KAITO: "Are you here to hurt someone?"

She turns. She studies him. It feels like being read by a machine.

SHIRA: "Not today."

KAITO: (opens his bento, splits the tamagoyaki) "Then eat something. The roof gets cold."

NARRATOR (SHIRA): I have been trained for interrogation. I have been trained for seduction, intimidation, and torture. I have been trained to read micro-expressions and anticipate aggression. I have not been trained for this.

NARRATOR (SHIRA): He is offering me half an egg roll. There is no tactical advantage to this. It is a waste of resources. It compromises his own caloric intake.

NARRATOR (SHIRA): I do not have a protocol for this.

CHOICE POINT
A) Accept the answer, ask nothing more. → SHIRA TRUST +15. Sets flag "Disarmed by Acceptance." She is confused by the lack of interrogation.
B) Ask who the target is. → SHIRA TRUST +5. She doesn't answer, but notes his perception.
C) "If it's Elia, I'll stop you." → SHIRA TRUST +20. Maximum gain. She looks at him for a very long time. Something shifts.

She takes the food. She eats it standing up. She does not say thank you. She does not leave.

Kaito finishes lunch and leaves. Shira remains on the roof for twenty minutes, sitting very still, looking at the empty space where the bento box used to be.

Chapter 2: "The Model and the Three-Second Calculation"

[BG: Photography Studio - Bright lights, equipment] [BGM: "Flashbulb" - rhythmic, electronic]

NARRATOR (KAITO): Airi asked me to carry equipment for a charity shoot her father's school is sponsoring. I said yes. I generally say yes to Airi. She has a way of making requests sound like logical inevitabilities.

The studio is chaotic. In the center, under the lights: Sora Mizuki. She enters the room and the air changes. She is wearing a dress that costs more than my house. She looks bored, tired, and spectacularly beautiful. She registers the crew, the lights, and me with the same professional detachment.

Her co-model, Shōta, steps into the frame. He is handsome in a way that suggests he checks mirrors every four minutes. He is annoyed.

[SFX: Shove sound]

Shōta shoulders Sora aside. It's subtle. Just enough to ruin her pose.

SHŌTA: (whispering) "Stop stealing the frame, doll."

Nobody moves. The director is checking lenses. The crew is tired and he is expensive. Sora freezes, her professional mask slipping for a fraction of a second. She prepares to apologize. She has done this a thousand times.

Kaito walks onto the set. He does not hurry. He does not look angry. He walks up to Shōta.

KAITO: "Don't touch her."

The silence that follows is absolute. Shōta blinks. He looks at Kaito. He looks at Kaito's eyes.

SHŌTA: "Who the hell are—"

KAITO: "I said don't touch her."

It isn't a threat. It is a statement of fact. Shōta steps back. The air in the room changes.

KAITO: (turning to Sora) "Are you alright?"

Sora looks at him. She has been modeled, styled, directed, managed, redirected, apologized to, and soothed since she was fourteen. Nobody has ever simply stopped it.

NARRATOR (SORA): Three seconds. That's how long it took. He didn't look at my dress. He didn't look at the camera. He looked at my face to check if I was scared.

SORA: "...I'm fine."

KAITO: "Good."

He walks back to the shadows.

[SCENE BREAK: The Car Ride Home]

Sora sits in the back of her car. She stares out the window. She pulls out her phone. She opens the event staff list. She finds the name "Amane." She stares at it for twenty minutes.

Airi catches up to Kaito outside the studio.

AIRI: "What happened back there?"

CHOICE POINT: Airi asks what happened
A) "Nothing. It was handled." → AIRI TRUST +5, SORA AFFECTION +8. Stoic response.
B) "Someone was being unkind. I said something." → AIRI AFFECTION +10, SORA AFFECTION +12. Honest response.
C) (Don't explain. Just keep walking.) → SORA AFFECTION +15, AIRI TRUST +8. Sora watches him leave. She asks her manager for his name immediately.

Chapter 3: "The Prince of Cakes"

[BG: Seiran Cafeteria] [BGM: "Daily Life" - upbeat]

Haruto Fujishima stands on a table. He is dramatic. He is holding a whisk like a scepter.

HARUTO: "I, Haruto Fujishima, the Prince of Patisserie, declare a rivalry!"

NARRATOR (KAITO): I just wanted to eat my lunch.

CLASSMATE A: "Oh god, he's doing it again."

CLASSMATE B: "Wait, who's he declaring war on? The new transfer?"

Elia is watching with rapt attention. She leans over to Airi.

ELIA: "Is this a duel to the death? Should I offer my sword?"

AIRI: "No swords, Elia. Just pastry."

YUI: (whispering to Hana) "Observe. The Prince has identified a rival. The rival does not know he is a rival. This is already my favorite arc."

HANA: "It's not an arc, Yui."

YUI: "It is absolutely an arc."

HARUTO: "Amane Kaito! I challenge you! A cooking duel! Winner takes the title of Seiran's Number One!"

KAITO: "...Number one what?"

HARUTO: "Of everything! Obviously!"

[SCENE BREAK: The Cooking Duel]

Kaito makes a simple omurice. He uses ingredients from the home ec fridge. Haruto makes a twelve-layer genoise cake with spun sugar lattice that looks like a cathedral.

Haruto takes one bite of Kaito's omurice. He freezes. He drops his fork. Tears form in his eyes. Genuine tears.

HARUTO: "It tastes... like home. Like a mother's love I never knew because my mother was always at board meetings."

KAITO: "Do you... want the rest?"

HARUTO: "Yes. You are my best friend now. We are best friends. I will die for you."

Kaito looks at Ryuusei, who is laughing so hard he can't breathe.

Shira watches from the back of the cafeteria. She writes in a small notebook.

LOG ENTRY 402: Subject neutralizes threats via high-calorie kindness. Strategy effective but confusing. Why does he not simply eliminate the opposition?

Chapter 4: "What Yui Sees"

[BG: School Corridor - Late Afternoon]

NARRATOR (KAITO): Yui Shinohara knows things. I don't know how she knows them. She just... knows. She is the girl who laughs at everything and takes nothing seriously. Or so it seems.

Kaito finds a notebook on the floor near the shoe lockers. It is labeled "OBSERVATIONS: CLASSIFIED." He picks it up. It falls open.

ENTRY 1: AIRI HARUNO. Brings him food. Reorganizes his schedule. Shows up at his house. DIAGNOSIS: Terminal. ETA: Six months.

ENTRY 2: HANA KAZATO. Has recalculated her route home 14 times this week. Challenged him to three races. DIAGNOSIS: Extremely terminal. ETA: Next confession window.

ENTRY 3: ELIA VON ASTRAEA. Already proposed. Is learning to use a smartphone to send him good morning messages. DIAGNOSIS: Beyond terminal. No ETA needed.

ENTRY 4: SHIROGANE SEIRA. Watches him. Does not initiate. Has turned down every social approach except his. DIAGNOSIS: Unknown quantity. Flagged.

Yui appears around the corner. She sees him holding the notebook. She snatches it. She is flushed.

YUI: "You saw nothing."

KAITO: "I saw some of it."

YUI: "You saw nothing. It was a weather report."

KAITO: "It was very detailed for a weather report."

She tries to make a joke. It falters.

YUI: "You should date all of them. Save yourself the indecision calories. It's efficient."

Kaito does not laugh. He looks at her steadily.

KAITO: "Yui-san. Do you want to say something?"

She opens her mouth. Closes it. Nothing comes.

YUI: "...I hate you a little bit right now."

KAITO: "I'll wait."

CHOICE POINT: Returning the notebook
A) "You have good instincts." → YUI AFFECTION +12. She tries to make a joke. It doesn't land.
B) "Some of this is wrong." → YUI AFFECTION +8. She demands corrections. He gives none. She is furious.
C) (Read the last page — the empty one — and hand it back.) → YUI AFFECTION +25, TRUST +15. The empty page is hers. She knows he knows. She adds an entry that night.

Later that night. Yui sits at her desk. She opens the notebook to the last page. She writes something. We don't see what it is. She closes the book more carefully than she ever has.

Chapter 5: "The Father's Study"

[BG: Chairman's Office - Opulent, intimidating] [BGM: "Authority" - deep strings]

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "Sit down, Amane-kun."

The office is larger than Kaito's house. Every object is chosen to intimidate.

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "Tea?"

KAITO: "Thank you."

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "I've done some research. Your background is... interesting. A sudden change in academic performance. A sudden change in physical capability. Your grandfather was a recluse."

KAITO: "He was a quiet man."

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "I have an offer. A scholarship. Full ride. University connections. A very comfortable future. Resources you currently lack."

KAITO: "And in exchange?"

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "You maintain an appropriate distance from my daughter. Airi has a destiny. She has responsibilities. You are... a complication. An attractive complication, perhaps, but a complication."

Kaito looks at the contract on the desk. He looks at the Chairman. He sees a father who thinks he is doing the right thing.

KAITO: "What does Airi-san want?"

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "I'm sorry?"

KAITO: "In this arrangement. What does she want?"

CHAIRMAN HARUNO: "She wants what is best for her future."

KAITO: "You're answering for her."

Silence. The Chairman stares at him.

KAITO: "With respect, Haruno-san — I'll make that decision with her, not for her."

CHOICE POINT: Exiting the meeting
A) Walk out calmly. → AIRI TRUST +10, CHAIRMAN RESPECT +15. The Chairman re-evaluates him.
B) "I decline." → AIRI AFFECTION +12. Formal and firm.
C) "I hope you ask her yourself sometime." → AIRI AFFECTION +20. Best response. The question lands hard.

The next day. Airi brings Kaito lunch. She doesn't mention the meeting. But she looks at her father differently that evening.

Chapter 6: "The Wire Drops"

[BG: School Festival Courtyard] [BGM: "Tension" - fast percussion]

NARRATOR (KAITO): I knew it would be today. The pattern. The roof access being unlocked. The festival noise masking movement. I've been watching her watch us all week.

Elia is walking through the crowd with Airi. She is laughing about a crepe. She looks happy.

From the roof: a glint. A wire. Invisible to everyone else. It extends twenty meters down, razor-sharp, aimed precisely at Elia's path.

Kaito moves. He doesn't step in front of Elia. He steps into the wire's path. Between Elia and the roof. He stops. He looks up.

On the roof, Shira freezes. Her finger is on the release. If she pulls, the wire cuts him. She has never failed a contract. The calculation takes 0.2 seconds.

NARRATOR (SHIRA): Kill him. Complete contract. Collateral acceptable. That is the protocol. That is the rule.

He looks at her. He shakes his head. Once. Sad. Not angry. Just... disappointed.

NARRATOR (SHIRA): I dropped it. I don't know why. I dropped the wire.

[SCENE BREAK: Behind the gym]

Shira descends. She stands before him and Elia.

KAITO: "How long have you been here?"

SHIRA: "Eighteen days."

KAITO: "You stopped."

SHIRA: "...Yes."

KAITO: "Why?"

SHIRA: "I don't know. I've been trying to calculate that for eleven seconds and I don't have an answer."

ELIA: "Come with us."

SHIRA: "I tried to kill you."

ELIA: "I know. You didn't. Come with us."

CHOICE POINT: Kaito's offer
A) "You made a choice. That counts." → SHIRA TRUST +20. Validating her agency.
B) "You were born into this. That doesn't make you it." → SHIRA TRUST +25. Validating her humanity.
C) (Hold out his hand. Say nothing.) → SHIRA AFFECTION +30. She looks at the hand. She looks at Kage. She takes it. The wire goes slack.

Chapter 7: "What You Were Born Into"
